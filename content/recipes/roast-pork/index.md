title: Roast Pork
date: 2019-01-08T12:08:50+10:30
tags:
    - Simple
    - Roast
ingredients:
    - Default:
    - 2kg leg of lamb, fat trimmed
    - 1/4 cup olive oil
    - 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary leaves
    - 2 garlic cloves, crushed
    - 1.5kg chat potatoes
    - Gravy:
    - 2 cups beef stock
    - 3/4 cup red wine
    - 2 1/2 tablespoons plain flour
method:
    - Default:
    - Preheat oven to 200째C/180째C fan-forced. Lightly grease roasting pan. Place lamb in pan. Combine oil, rosemary and garlic in a bowl. Rub half the oil mixture over lamb. Season with salt and pepper. Roast for 15 minutes.
    - Reduce oven temperature to 180째C/160째C fan-forced. Roast lamb, basting with remaining oil mixture every 20 minutes, for 1 hour 15 minutes for medium or until cooked to your liking. Add potatoes to pan for last 40 minutes, turning halfway through cooking.
    - Remove lamb from oven. Cover loosely with foil. Stand for 10 minutes. Carve. Serve with potatoes.
    - Bake for 15-18 minutes or until light golden. Set aside to cool slightly on the trays before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

    - Gravy:
    - Transfer meat (and any vegetables) to a plate to rest. Combine stock and wine in a jug. Skim fat from roasting pan, leaving 1 1/2 tablespoons pan juices and fat in pan. Place pan over high heat.
    - Add flour. Cook, stirring with a wooden spoon, for 1 to 2 minutes or until mixture bubbles and becomes golden. 
    - Add juices from resting meat. Slowly add stock mixture to pan, stirring constantly.
    - Cook, scraping pan, for 8 to 10 minutes or until thickened.
